JM Drill Draw — Quick Start

Animate mode

  1. Pick a skater from Players and click the ice to place them.
  2. In Move → Style, pick Skate. Click the player, then click waypoints for where they go. Double-click or Enter to finish.
  3. Press Play — the player glides along the path.
  4. For a puck, pick Pass style, click the carrier's spot, add waypoints, finish. The puck follows on Play.

2D Draw mode

Toggle 2D Draw at the top. Paths become static diagram lines — great for printing or whiteboard-style planning.

Speed & timing

Select a path, then use Start delay and Travel time in the panel that appears bottom-right. Hit ⇥ Stagger to automatically space out multiple players.

Save your drills

Click My Drills to name and save drills inside the app — open them any time. Use ⬇ Export to download a backup file.
