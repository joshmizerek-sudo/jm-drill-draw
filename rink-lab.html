Rink Lab — quick start

A whiteboard that moves. Drop pieces on the ice, draw where they travel, press play.

Make a piece move

  1. Click a skater (or any piece) in the tray, then click the ice to place it.
  2. Pick the Move tool and drag from that piece to draw where it travels. You'll see a shaded lane showing the range, with a faded ghost of the piece at the destination.
  3. Press Play. The piece glides along the lane. Give several pieces lanes and they move together.
  4. Reshape any lane: Select it and drag the dots. Move the piece and its lane follows.
  5. Want a rush that unfolds in order? Hit ⇥ Stagger, or select a lane and set its Start delay and Travel time.

The Skate, Pass, Shot, Arrow and Pen tools draw diagram lines only — they never move anything. Use Move for animation.

Carry & pass a puck

  1. Give your players their Move routes first.
  2. Select a puck. In its panel, Set carrier and click the player who starts with it.
  3. Add pass and click the receiver. The puck rides the carrier, then passes to where the receiver will be (a lead pass), then rides them. Chain more passes for give-and-go, D-to-D, breakouts.
  4. Adjust each pass's timing with its slider so it lands on the tape.

Build the picture

  1. Equipment tray: nets, pucks, cones, tires, bumpers, dots, goalie, coach.
  2. View isolates a zone (D-zone / neutral / O-zone). 2 rinks runs split-ice stations.
  3. ＋ Image drops in a client logo or photo; drag and resize it like any piece.
  4. Select tool: click to pick one, drag a box over empty ice to select many, Shift-click to add or remove. Then drag any to move them together, recolour, ⌘/Ctrl D to duplicate, or Delete to remove the whole group.

Keep your work

Save downloads the drill as a file; Open loads it back. Space play, Esc deselect, Ctrl/⌘ Z undo.
